Space Needs Guardians!

Our beautiful Space is getting messy! Just like your room needs cleaning, the space around Earth needs our help too. Want to join our team of Space Guardians? There are thousands of bits and pieces floating around our Planet, and we are on a mission to learn about them and help keep Space clean for all our future adventures.

What Do Space Guardians Do?

We are Earth's special team of Space protectors! Long ago, humans started sending Satellites into Space. Satellites help us watch cartoons and talk to friends far away. But sometimes, Satellites break or get old, leaving pieces floating around Earth like sprinkles on a birthday cake!

Space Guardians like you learn about messy Space and help tell other friends about keeping Space clean. We use Science, our Imagination, and Teamwork to understand how we can protect our Planet's Space neighborhood.

Remember: Even the smallest Space Guardian can make the biggest difference! Every time you learn something new about space and share it with a friend, you're helping our mission!

Fun Space Facts!

  • Space has over 100 million tiny pieces floating around... That is more sprinkles than on all the cupcakes in the world!
  • Some Space pieces zoom around Earth faster than 100 race cars put together!
  • If we lined up all the Space pieces, they could reach to the Moon and back 5 times!
  • Every year, about 200 new Space things go up... that's like adding a whole classroom of space toys!
